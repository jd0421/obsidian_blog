https://solvesql.com/problems/tip-and-revenue/

```MYSQL
select

  day

  , sum(tip) as `tip_daily`

  , sum(total_bill) as `revenue_daily`

  , round(sum(tip) * 100.0 / sum(total_bill), 2) as `tip_revenue_pct`

from tips

group by 1

order by 4 desc

;
```
